Hi, I'm Thio! (he/him)

I'm a fresh graduate in Physics from Universitas Padjadjaran (Indonesia ) with research internship experience in climate and energy transition.


My interest lie in the climate variability and the broader impacts of climate change, particularly in understanding how large-scale climate processes (such as ENSO) and climate extremes impact regional environments. In addition, I have a profound interest in energy transition issues, where I worked on the Energy Modelling project at IESR. I am also increasingly drawn to data science and machine learning, which I see as powerful tools to mitigate the uncertainty in climate research.


I have been a member of Pajajaran Physical Society community since 2023 and I'm passionate about communicating complex scientific concepts in an accessible way.

Research

Research Interest

ENSO Dynamics

I am interested in ENSO dynamics, particularly the physical mechanisms, such as equatorial wave processes, thermocline feedbacks, and coupled ocean-atmosphere interactions, as well as their far-reaching climate impacts through global teleconnections.

Climate Extremes

My interest centers on understanding the future changes in climate extremes, especially compound events, and examining how these extremes manifest and impact communities at regional scales. I aim to investigate both the underlying processes and the spatial patterns of risk associated with a warming climate.
